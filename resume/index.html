About Me

I received the B.S. degree from the Shandong University of Science and Technology, in 2016, and the M.S. degree from Shandong University of Science and Technology, in 2020. I am currently a researcher and NLP algorithm engineer with the AI Research Institute, Shumei Technology, China. My main research interests include Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning and Knowledge Graph. Also, I do some research about Process Mining, such as next event prediction. I focus on using AI to solve practical problems. I am actively seeking the opportunity of PhD(I’m more in the NLP direction but I’m also open to all other opportunities) and I can fund myself.
